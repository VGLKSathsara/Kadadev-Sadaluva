Welcome to Kadadev Sadaluva

Located in the heart of Kataragama New Town, just minutes away from the sacred Kataragama Devalaya and Kirivehera Temple. Kadadev Sadaluva offers clean, comfortable accommodation for pilgrims, families, and safari enthusiasts.

We provide both air-conditioned and non-air-conditioned rooms, plus two large function halls ideal for family gatherings, events, or group stays. Spacious parking, nearby supermarkets, restaurants, and easy access to Yala & Weheragala National Parks make us the perfect base for your stay.
