Welcome to Kadadev Sadaluva

Located in the heart of Kataragama New Town, just minutes from the sacred Kataragama Devalaya and Kirivehera Temple. We offer clean, comfortable AC and non-AC rooms plus spacious halls — perfect for pilgrims, families, and safari travelers.

Spacious parking, nearby shops, restaurants, and easy access to Yala & Weheragala make us your ideal base.
